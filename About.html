About:

Guitars Galore is a website where people can learn all of the information they want about guitars of every make and models, even the different accessories that are used when playing a guitar. People can learn of all models that have ever been made in the world of guitars from companies including Martin, Fender, Ibanez, Silvertone, and many more; along with all of the accessories that come with playing a guitar. Accessories includes amplifiers, capos, picks, tuners, etc.
